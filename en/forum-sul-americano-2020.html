BUILDING A CIRCULAR ECONOMY IN LATIN AMERICA
WCEFONLINE SIDE EVENT

Fostering the implementation of the circular economy globally requires attention to regional diversities, allowing an inclusive transition to this new economic model.

With this in mind, FIESP, CIESP and SENAI-SP organize a debate on the opportunities and challenges to establish a circular economy in Latin America and the importance of partnerships, with representatives from the public and private sectors.

Come and discuss with us the main paths to circularity in our continent!

The meeting will be held on the second day of activities of the World Circular Economy Forum Online (WCEF), an event organized by the Finnish Innovation Fund, SITRA, which brings together the main thinkers and executors of the circular economy from around the world.
